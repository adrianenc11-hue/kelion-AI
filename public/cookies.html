1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help remember your preferences and keep you logged in. We also use localStorage, a similar browser technology, to store preferences locally on your device.

3. Cookie Categories

  • Essential (Strictly Necessary): Required for the site to function. Cannot be disabled. Includes authentication, GDPR consent, and security tokens.
  • Functional: Enhance your experience (e.g., remembering preferences). Can be disabled.
  • Analytics: We do NOT currently use analytics cookies (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel).
  • Advertising: We do NOT use advertising or tracking cookies. Zero.
